Pancake

A pancake is a flat cake, often thin and round, prepared from a starch-based batter that may contain eggs, milk and butter and cooked on a hot surface such as a griddle or frying pan, often frying with oil or butter. It is a type of batter bread.

Ingredients

  • 77g cake flour
  • 5g baking powder
  • 25g sugar
  • salt
  • 1 egg
  • 97g milk

Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients and whisk till no visible powders
  2. Heat skillet in medium-low heat
  3. Pour a spoon of batter. Wait 2-3 min till see small bubbles on the edges. Flip and cook for another 2 min
  4. Can serve with maple syrup, butter, or fruits you like
